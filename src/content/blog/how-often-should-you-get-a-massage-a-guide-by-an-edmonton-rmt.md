## **Does Massage Need to Be Painful to Work?**

No. Massage therapy does not need to be painful to be effective.

Pressure is always adjusted based on your comfort, treatment goals, and how your body responds. Many clients find consistent, moderate-pressure massage more effective than occasional intense sessions.

## **How Lifestyle Affects Massage Frequency**

Your ideal massage schedule may depend on factors such as:

- Desk-based or physical work  
- Stress levels  
- Physical activity or exercise habits  
- Cold weather affecting muscle stiffness  
- Previous injuries  

Many Edmonton clients benefit from adjusting massage frequency during colder months when muscles tend to feel tighter.

## **Can Massage Therapy Be Preventative?**

Yes. Many people use massage therapy as preventative care.

Regular massage can help:
- Reduce the risk of muscle strain  
- Improve posture and movement  
- Manage stress before it becomes chronic  
- Support long-term physical health  

Preventative care often requires less intense treatment over time.
