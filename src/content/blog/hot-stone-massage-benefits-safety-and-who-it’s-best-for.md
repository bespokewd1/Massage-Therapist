## **What Is Hot Stone Massage?**

Hot stone massage is a therapeutic massage technique that uses smooth, heated stones‚Äîtypically basalt stones‚Äîplaced on specific areas of the body or used during massage strokes.

## **How Hot Stone Massage Works**

The warmth from the stones gently penetrates muscles and connective tissue. This helps:

- Increase circulation  
- Reduce muscle stiffness  
- Promote relaxation  
- Improve flexibility  

Because muscles relax faster with heat, the therapist can often work more effectively without needing excessive pressure.

## **Who Is Hot Stone Massage Best For?**

Hot stone massage may be a good option if you:

- Feel muscle stiffness or tightness  
- Have difficulty relaxing during massage  
- Experience stress or tension in your body  
- Prefer warmth during treatments  
- Live with cold-related muscle discomfort  

Your RMT will assess whether hot stone massage is appropriate for you.

## **Is Hot Stone Massage Safe?**

When performed by a Registered Massage Therapist, hot stone massage is safe for most people.

However, it may not be recommended if you have:

- Certain medical conditions  
- Sensitivity to heat  
- Acute inflammation  
- Recent injuries  

Your RMT will always adjust the treatment or suggest alternatives if needed.
